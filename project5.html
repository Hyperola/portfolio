Fursa energy

UI/UX Design

Overview

Fursa Energy is an e-commerce platform selling engine oil and related products. The platform provides a user-friendly marketplace for customers to browse and purchase energy products.

The Challenge

Creating an intuitive, visually appealing UI/UX for Fursa Energy to simplify product discovery and purchase for users, while ensuring a seamless shopping experience.

My Role

  • UI/UX Design: Crafted an intuitive, visually appealing interface using Figma, focusing on clear navigation and product presentation.
  • Prototyping: Developed interactive prototypes to validate user flows and design decisions.
  • User Research: Conducted research to align the design with customer needs and preferences.

Process

User Research: Analyzed target audience needs to inform design decisions.

Wireframing and Prototyping: Created wireframes and high-fidelity prototypes in Figma to map user flows and interactions.

Design Iteration: Refined designs based on feedback to enhance usability and aesthetics.

Collaboration: Worked with stakeholders to ensure the design aligned with brand identity and business goals.

Challenges

Balancing Aesthetics and Functionality: Ensuring the design was visually engaging while maintaining ease of use for diverse customers.

User Flow Optimization: Simplifying complex product filtering and checkout processes for a seamless experience.

Results

  • A user-friendly, responsive UI/UX design for Fursa Energy’s e-commerce platform.
  • Improved product discoverability and streamlined purchase process.
  • Positive stakeholder feedback on design usability and brand alignment.

What I Learned

UI/UX Design: Enhanced skills in creating intuitive e-commerce interfaces.

User-Centered Design: Deepened understanding of aligning designs with user needs and business objectives.

Prototyping: Improved proficiency in Figma for rapid prototyping and iteration.
