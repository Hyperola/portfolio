Hoodit

Front-end Development

Overview

Hoodit is a student focused rental and marketplace web app. it connects students with verified landlords and sellers offfering secure housing and a trusted space to buy/sell essentials.

User Research

Method: Surveys, interviews and community observation.

Key Insights:

Students struggle to find affordable verified listings

Fear of scams makes them wary of online transactions.

There's a demand for a student-only trusted platforms.

Problem Statement

Students need a safe, reliable way to find housing and buy/sell goods within thier campus communities.

Design Process

Personas: Developed 3 personas: "Busy Student," "Freelance Creative," and "Tech-Savvy Professional."

Wireframes: Sketched low-fidelity wireframes in Figma, focusing on a tabbed interface for quick toggling.

Prototyping: Built high-fidelity prototypes, tested with 12 users, and iterated based on feedback—e.g., simplified the onboarding flow.

Visual Design: Used Slate Gray as the base with orange accents (#F28C38) for vibrancy, ensuring a modern, approachable feel.

Development Process

Tech Stack: React.js,Node.js, MongoDB, Express.js, Google Auth, Vercel.

Features: Verified house listings and sellers profiles

Secure Login (Google)

Real-time chat & Map-based Filtering

Whilst & favorites

User reviews & ratings

Challenges

Challenge: Design a seamless user experience that combine house Listings and market place without overwhelming users.

Solution: I simplified the navigation by seperating both features into clear categories , i used intuitive icons, consisitent color schemes and clear CTAs to guide users. User flows were streamlined, and i focused on minimal layouts to ensure clarity and ease of use.
